    General Trias College of Cavite is dedicated to providing higher education that empowers individuals to become leaders in their professions and communities. Our academic offerings are structured into two main divisions: the College for undergraduate studies and the Graduate School for advanced professional development. We focus on rigorous academic training, research capability, and ethical leadership to prepare our students for the challenges of a globalized world.
